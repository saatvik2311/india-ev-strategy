2. Demand Forecasting Math
Data: 5 years (2020-2024) of historical EV registrations.
Algorithm: Technology adoption follows an S-Curve. Because we are in the mass adoption phase, growth is exponential: Y = A * e^(rt).
Transformation: We take the natural log to make it linear: ln(Y) = ln(A) + r*t.
Execution: We use Ordinary Least Squares (OLS) regression (np.polyfit) to find the slope (r), which represents the Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR).
Guardrails: CAGR is strictly clipped between 5% and 120% to prevent mathematical impossibilities in small states. Fallback CAGR is 35% if data points are <2.
